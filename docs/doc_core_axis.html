Initialice an instance of an axis

Parameters:
  • point_1 – Point-object or (y,x)-tuple
  • point_2 – Point-object or (y,x)-tuple
  • azimuth – azimuth in gon if only one point is given
  • orientation – angle in gon
  • left_right – distance in meter to displace the axis to the left or right
  • left_right_through_point – Point-object or (y,x)-tuple
  • plumb – turn the axis with 100gon
  • plumb_through_point – Point-object or (y,x)-tuple
  • plumb_trough_middle – boolean -> builds perpendicular bisector
  • in_out – distance in meter do displace axis in line
